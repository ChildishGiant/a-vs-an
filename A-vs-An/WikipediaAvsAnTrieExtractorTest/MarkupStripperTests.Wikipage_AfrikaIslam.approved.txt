"Afrika Islam" (born Charles Glenn, 1968, New York City) also known as the ""Son of Bambaataa"", is an American hip-hop producer . 

Life
In the 1980s he moved to Los Angeles where he co-produced Ice T's early albums "Rhyme Pays" and "Power".
In the late 1990s, Afrika Islam joined German techno icon Westbam to form Mr. X and Mr. Y, a techno duo that made commercial techno with Electro influences.
